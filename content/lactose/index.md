Lactose is a natural sugar found in milk and dairy products. It's the primary carbohydrate for cow (4-5% body weight).


### Why some people are lactose intolerant?

In our small intestine, there is an enzyme called lactase. Lactase breaks down lactose to smaller form of sugar: glucose and galactose. 

Lactase production decreases as we age, leading to lactose intolerance. 

Lactose intolerance leading to digestive problem like bloating and diarrhea.

Lactose intolerance is the **default state**, not a disorder. In history, thousands of years ago, our ancestors didn't consume milk. Our body are adapting to what we consume.

### Random facts

- 65-70% adults in the world population have lactose intolerance.

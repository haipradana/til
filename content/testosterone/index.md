### What is testosterone?

Testosterone is a hormone that is produced in testicles (for male) and ovaries (for female). It's something that builds male characteristics---muscles, bone, deep voice, body hair. It also affects sex drive and mood.

### Key functions

- It increases basal metabolic rate (BMR)
- Increases sexual drive (libido)
- Promotes sperm production and the growth of penis during puberty
- Better mood and confidence.


### What if a male has low testosterone?

- Linked to depression, fatigue, and irritability (sensian)
- Erectile dysfunction
- Infertility
- Reduced muscle mass

But high testosterone can leads to:

- Acne
- Agression
- Prostate enlargement
- Muscle mass increase
- Faster body hair growth
- Deepening voice

### Testosterone production

There are many factors that impact the production of testosterone:

- Sleep
- Diet 
- Exercise
- Stress
- Zinc
- Vitamin D


### Does woman have testosterone? 

Yes, but only 5% to 10% of what men have.

### Random facts

- Testosterone term came from "testicles" and "sterone" or steroid ketone.
- Bodybuilders can abuse the usage of steroids to significantly improve testosterone that leads to muscle growth, but it can affect fertility, heart issues, and liver damage.

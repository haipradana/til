There is several hormones are working when we're feel happy. Different cause, different hormones. It can divided by 4 hormones that control the "happy feelings" of human:

1. Dopamine

Dopamine trigger feel of pleasure, satisfaction, and motivation. It's like "reward" system in our brains. 

Dopamine released when we achieve a goal or complete a task. So, if we have some a big problem, we can break it into several task in order to manipulate our brain to increase dopamine after complete one small task, every day.

2. Serotonin

Serotonin helps regulate our mood, sleep, appetite, and digestion.

It creates a sense of well-being, calm, and focus. A lack of serotonin deeply correlated with depression and anxiety.

3. Oxytosin

Exytosis promotes and increase a trust, empathy, and bonding in relationship

It will increase through physical affection like hugging, kissing, or sex. Oxytosin helps build the "long-term" happines that assosiated with security and connection through a relationship.

4. Endorphins

This is our natural painkiller. Endorphins "control" our natural response to stress or physical pain.

They produce ouphoric feelings like "high". It can reach from activities like power lifting, exercise, laughter, or even eating spicy food.

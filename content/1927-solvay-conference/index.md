This is the most famous picture of physics, the 1927 solvay conference held in Brussels, Belgium. The initiator is Ernest Solvay. This is a turning point for the physics world where physicists discussed the new quantum theory. Many notable physicists attended this event and around 60% (17/29) of the attendees won noble prize.

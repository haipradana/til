Dasarian is unit of time commonly used on meteorology and climatology domain, consist of 10-days. In other words, **dasarian** is a *10-days period*.

BMKG using dasarian as a standard because it can monitor weather and climate variability much better than monthly, but not too short like daily.

- BMKG use dasarian for **determine beginning of a season** as a scientific indicators when dry season or wet season begin on a region.

- Dasarian is a effective way to monitoring pattern of atmospheric phenomenona that change gradually, such as *moonson wind* or *drought index*.

- Dasarian help farmers to planting period and irrigation planning because it gave description of groundwater availability much precision than monthly mean.
